COMMENT SEEDING STRATEGY

Samsung Galaxy A57 5G
Turning Negativity into Engagement

Strategic comment seeding across Instagram, Reddit, and Twitter (X) to shift audience perception, dilute negative sentiment, and reinforce Samsung's brand narrative.

THE CHALLENGE

Negative sentiment
left unaddressed

Negative audience sentiment — pricing concerns, Apple comparisons, brand bias — can significantly impact overall product perception and brand image. Highly visible critical feedback shapes audience opinion and decision-making, leading to hesitation and reduced trust even when the majority sentiment is positive.

20%
Negative Comments
18%
Price Sensitivity
16%
Apple Comparison
3
Platforms Managed
SOLUTION

Comment Seeding Strategy

Strategically placing relevant, positive, and informative responses within existing comment threads to guide and influence conversations.

🌱
Deploy Seeding
Consistently highlight product strengths and positive experiences within high-traffic comment sections across Instagram, Reddit, and Twitter.
🔄
Contextual Replies
Use contextual replies to shift narrative and reinforce positive sentiment — actively building conversations around key features within existing threads.
📌
Amplify Positives
Amplify and pin positive comments to boost visibility and sentiment. Counter-position Apple comparisons with "iPhone-like camera under ₹20K" narrative.
KEY LEARNINGS

Insights & Takeaways

Strategic Moves
  • Meme → Proof Layering: meme content supported by gaming tests and camera comparisons addressing the 14% feature-seeking audience.
  • Smart Comment Mining: user queries like "Battery?" and "Camera?" turned into reels and comparison videos with pinned conversion hooks.
  • Comment-driven conversion triggers: pinned comments reinforced "flagship features under ₹2K/month" to drive consideration.
Key Learnings
  • Negative feedback, if not addressed, can influence overall perception despite strong positive volume.
  • Timely intervention through strategic responses and narrative building is crucial to steer audience sentiment effectively.
  • High engagement-driven content naturally attracts mixed sentiment, making active conversation management essential.
